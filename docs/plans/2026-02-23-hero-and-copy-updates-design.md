# Design Proposal: Hero, Success Page & "How It Works" Updates

## Overview
This proposal details the implementation of the "Architectural Premium" messaging angle combined with the new typographic hierarchy and custom animated SVG assets, aligning with the `BRAND_IDENTITY.md` specifications.

## 1. Typographic Hierarchy Update

*   **Headlines (`font-editorial` / `font-display`):** Integrate `domainedispnar-medium` for high-impact headers (Hero, Success Page, Section Headers). We will leverage italics for emphasis.
*   **Body Copy (`font-body`):** Implement `Raela Pro` in Light (300) and Extra Light (200) for general body text to increase the airy, premium feel. Large lead paragraphs will use Medium (500).
*   **Eyebrows & Accents (`font-structural`):** Utilize `Syne` sparingly for small section labels, eyebrow copy (e.g., above the hero), and structural tags to anchor the design architecturally.

## 2. Hero Section Refinement

**Messaging Strategy:** Architectural Premium focusing on the "Crafted" approach.

**Copy Proposal:**
*   **Eyebrow (Syne):** THE CRAFTED APPROACH
*   **Headline (Domaine):** The *Crafted* Blueprint.
*   **Subheadline (Raela):** An architectural foundation for high-performance digital experiences. Eliminate guesswork before development even begins.

**Visual Asset (Custom SVG Idea):**
Instead of a standard icon, we will implement a custom SVG that echoes the "Success Page" animation style (delicate, glowing rings, scanning lines, or a subtle blueprint schematic unfolding).
*   **Concept:** A minimalist, abstracted wireframe or a series of concentric, slow-rotating rings (gold/amber) layered over a faint grid, symbolizing "structure" and "precision." It should fade in with a cinematic ease.

## 3. "How It Works" Section Overhaul (The 3-D Framework)

**Messaging Strategy:** We will structure the "How It Works" section exactly around the Discovery, Design, Delivery framework: a definitive framework for digital authority.

**Copy Proposal:**
*   **Eyebrow (Syne):** A DEFINITIVE FRAMEWORK
*   **Step 1: Discovery**
    *   *Description:* We examine your current digital infrastructure to identify where momentum is lost, auditing every touchpoint for clarity and conversion impact.
    *   *Bullets:* Strategic gap analysis • Revenue leak identification • Audience resonance audit
*   **Step 2: Design**
    *   *Description:* We interpret your unique value into a visual language that commands authority, building a system-level architecture that scales with your ambition.
    *   *Bullets:* Brand cosmology development • System-level interface design • Cinematic visual production
*   **Step 3: Deliver**
    *   *Description:* A production-ready ecosystem handed over with complete operational clarity, ensuring you can lead your market without technical overhead.
    *   *Bullets:* Full-stack implementation • Performance optimization • Operational handoff training

**Visual Assets (Custom SVG Ideas):**
*   **Discovery:** An abstracted eye or scanning reticle, perhaps with a slow pulse or scanline effect indicating "analysis."
*   **Design:** Interlocking geometric shapes or a drafting compass motif, drawn in with delayed SVG path animation.
*   **Deliver:** A solid, completed architectural form (like a glowing monolith or geometric cube) with a subtle upward drift or light-ray effect.
